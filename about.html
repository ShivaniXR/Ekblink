About us

One blink.

Ek Blink Solutions is a creative marketing agency specialising in immersive 360-degree videos, AR/VR content, and digital innovation. We blend technology and storytelling to elevate brands through video, design, marketing, web, and mobile solutions.

In Hindi, ek blink literally means one blink. That's all any brand gets before someone decides whether to keep looking or keep scrolling. The AR that stops people mid-scroll. The 360 campaign that makes a room feel like somewhere else. Every part of it, handled in-house.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionise brand storytelling through immersive technology, delivering impactful marketing solutions powered by 360-degree video, augmented and virtual reality, and digital innovation that connect, engage, and inspire audiences across the globe.
